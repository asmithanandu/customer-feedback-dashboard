Actionable insights
CSAT improved 11 points in 6 months

Consistent agent training and script adherence are paying off. Maintain weekly feedback calibration sessions.

Support stage satisfaction lowest at 54%

Customers rate complaint handling poorly. Recommend reducing average hold time and improving empathy training for agents.
